contentdrip.io

In 2021, I wanted to challenge myself to create something that had a real practical purpose. As a hobby photographer, I know that posting to social media can be painful especially if you're posting to multiple social platforms at once. This app was designed to make it as easy as possible for photographers and other content producers to upload a large amount of images, set minimal amounts of captions, hashtags, and set other parameters in which the backend would run through it creating all of the scheduled posts and publish them to a third party API.

Stack: Angular/Python/AWS/MongoDB/auth0

Command & Control

Many operations manage things from spreadsheets - from small businesses to Fortune 500. We know that managing real-time operations can be very difficult from a spreadsheet! Previously, I had developed a dispatch and fleet dashboard on Google sheets with some basic automation for the company. After having gained experience through engineering as a hobby, I felt it was time to make a scalable application for my company.

So, I created a full stack application that allows the organization to maintain status of all vehicles, maintenance notes, employee scheduling and management, vehicle reporting, and so much more. The app is currently being used at one of the highest performing accounts at the company and has become a critical tool that has boosted productivity and communication. This has been a fantastic project for me because I was able to build something practical and through operational demands and user feedback, I've been able to make improvements to make the app better and better.

Stack: Angular/Python/AWS/MongoDB/auth0
